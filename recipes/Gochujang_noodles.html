Gochujang Noodles Recipe

The Gochujang Noodles recipe is a delicious and flavorful dish that combines the heat of gochujang paste with the satisfying texture of noodles. This Korean-inspired recipe features a medley of fresh vegetables, such as carrots, bell peppers, and green onions, along with your choice of protein, such as tofu, chicken, or beef. The noodles are cooked to perfection and then tossed in a mouthwatering sauce made from gochujang paste, soy sauce, sesame oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger. The dish is garnished with sesame seeds and chopped green onions, adding a delightful crunch and freshness. With its bold flavors and vibrant colors, Gochujang Noodles are sure to be a hit at any mealtime.

Ingredients

  • Gochujang paste
  • Noodles
  • Vegetables (e.g., carrots, bell peppers, green onions)
  • Protein (e.g., tofu, chicken, beef)
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a pot and cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a separate pan, heat some oil and saut√© the vegetables until they are tender.
  3. Add the protein of your choice and cook until it is fully cooked.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together gochujang paste, soy sauce, sesame oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger.
  5. Add the cooked noodles to the pan with the vegetables and protein. Pour the gochujang sauce over the noodles and toss everything together until well coated.
  6. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
